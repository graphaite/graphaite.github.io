MISSION

Imagined in Switzerland; implemented in Cambridge.

Graphaite was born out of a passion for graph neural networks and their applications.

APPLICATIONS

Inspired by the work of Michael Bronstein at Twitter and Tobias Pfaff & Peter Battaglia at DeepMind, Graphaite will focus on researching exciting problems and implementing graph neural networks (GNNs) to solve these.

Active areas of research range from improving engineering designs using GNN-based learned simulators to causal graph discovery from time-series data.
